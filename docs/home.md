# The Osprey

A living record of hardware and software steps — from first schematic to final build

## About Elijah Brown and Kelton Jensen

This duo of Elijah, being a mechanical engineer, and Kelton, being an electrical engineer, has come together with a vision. An osprey drone. The goal of our project is to manufacture from the ground up an osprey-like drone with a custom-made exterior hub and main + controller PCB.

## Project Summaries

### Page One — Step Alpha
The Initial Planning page consists of pure planning, consisting of component selection, desired variables achievable, budget, and initial design.

### Page Two — Step Beta
The CAD-Schematics page consists of the 3D model of the Osprey and its defining characteristics.

### Page Three — Step Gamma
The PCB page consists of the PCB schematic, KiCAD PCB files, and its functions.
