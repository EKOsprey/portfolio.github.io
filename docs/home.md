# The Osprey

A living record of hardware and software steps — from first schematic to final build

## About Elijah Brown and Kelton Jensen

This duo of Elijah, being a mechanical engineer, and Kelton, being an electrical engineer, has come together with a vision. An osprey drone. The goal of our project is to manufacture from the ground up an osprey-like drone with a custom-made exterior hub and main + controller PCB.
